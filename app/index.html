InputScope

Mouse and keyboard input heatmap visualizer, with statistics.

Overview

InputScope is a powerful tool that visualizes your mouse and keyboard input patterns through heatmaps and provides detailed statistics about your usage habits.

Features

  • Real-time Heatmap Visualization: See where you click and type most frequently
  • Detailed Statistics: Track your input patterns over time
  • Customizable Display: Adjust visualization settings to your preference
  • Cross-platform Support: Works on Windows, macOS, and Linux
